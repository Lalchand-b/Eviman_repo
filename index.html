Quidrix

QUADRIX is a groundbreaking innovation by Eviman Transport Pvt. Ltd., designed to redefine sustainable mobility in Bharat. Powered by a unique hybrid system that integrates Electric, Hydrogen, Solar, and Biofuel energy sources, QUADRIX delivers unmatched versatility and efficiency. Built on our proprietary dual powertrain mechanism, it ensures reliable performance across varied terrains while remaining eco-friendly and cost-effective. Positioned in the L7M segment, QUADRIX offers the safety and comfort of a car while maintaining the affordability and efficiency of a three-wheeler. With our in-house safety innovations and SCAB system, it prioritizes passenger protection without compromising on cost. Affordable, sustainable, and future-ready, QUADRIX is set to be the most accessible hybrid vehicle of its kind, making clean mobility a reality for every Indian.
